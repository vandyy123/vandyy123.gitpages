lil' Elaine

Lil' Elaine started out like every single one of us; just pure nothingness. But one day lil' Elaine came to be as real Elaine made her for her sister Kathleen for Kathleen's birthday. Since her inception into this world, lil' Elaine has been by Kathleen's side, and a part of her inanimate family, offering love, support, and comfort.
